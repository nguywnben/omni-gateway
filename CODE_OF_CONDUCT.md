# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in Omni Gateway respectful, inclusive, and free from harassment for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or level of technical expertise.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and specific.
- Assume good intent while addressing the technical substance of disagreements.
- Accept feedback gracefully and correct mistakes openly.
- Protect private information, credentials, and security reports.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the project and its community.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized content, deliberate intimidation, doxxing, credential disclosure, sustained disruption, and personal attacks are not tolerated in project spaces or related private communication.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through the contact options available on the owner's GitHub profile. Reports are reviewed confidentially and in good faith. Maintainers may edit or remove content, issue warnings, temporarily restrict participation, or permanently ban participants when necessary to protect the community.

## Scope

This policy applies in repository issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews, and other spaces where someone is representing the project.

This Code of Conduct is informed by the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct.html), version 2.1.
